overnight oats.

makes 1 serving

ingredients

  • •rolled oats
  • •goat milk
  • •chia seeds
  • •almond butter
  • •granola
  • •berries (straw, rasp, blue, etc.)

instructions

  1. 1. pour about 1/2 cup of oats into sealable container.
  2. 2. cover oats with goat milk.
  3. 3. add a spoonful of chia seeds and stir.
  4. 4. mix in a spoonful of almond butter. just spread it out a bit easy.
  5. 5. refrigerate overnight.
  6. 6. stir to fluff up the oats. optional: nuke for 45-60 seconds.
  7. 7. top with granola and sliced strawberries (or whatever berries).
