kale smoothie bowl.

makes 1 serving

ingredients

  • •1 frozen banana
  • •4 chunks of frozen pineapple
  • •a handful of frozen kale
  • •a splash of goat milk
  • •granola
  • •almond butter
  • •sliced strawberries

instructions

  1. 1. blend banana, pineapple and kale until no big chunks remain.
  2. 2. add goat milk and blend until smooth.
  3. 3. pour over a spoonful of granola.
  4. 4. top with more granola, almond butter, and strawberries.
