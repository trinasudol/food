avocado toast.

makes 1 serving

ingredients

  • •1 tsp butter
  • •1/4 large avocado
  • •1 egg
  • •a ~splash~ of goat milk
  • •1 piece of bread (only the heartiest, please)
  • •salt and pepper
  • •olive oil
  • •everything bagel seasoning (trader joe's !)

instructions

  1. 2. lightly mash avocado.
  2. 3. beat egg. add goat milk and beat more.
  3. 5. toast bread. crispy.
  4. 6. melt butter in nonstick pan over low heat.
  5. 7. add egg to pan. stir it up with chopsticks.
  6. 8. once egg has set slightly, stir again with chopsticks to form small curds. don't full on scramble now.
  7. 9. add salt and pepper to avocado and mash some more to combine.
  8. 10. salt and pepper egg. scramble it (or roll halfway for omelet) until set. remove from heat (and finish rolling).
  9. 11. drizzle olive oil over toast. spread avocado. sprinkle seasoning. top with egg.
  10. 12. eat.
