apple oatmeal.

makes 1 serving

ingredients

  • •1/2 an apple
  • •1 tsp butter
  • •rolled oats
  • •goat milk
  • •almond butter

instructions

  1. 1. dice apple. heat butter in frying pan over medium heat.
  2. 2. sauteé apples.
  3. 3. cover about 1/2 cup oats with goat milk and nuke for 2 minutes.
  4. 4. stir in almond butter and apples.
  5. 5. eat.
