cupcakes.

makes 12 cupcakes

ingredients

  • •3 cups almond flour
  • •1/2 tsp baking soda
  • •1/2 tsp salt
  • •1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  • •1/2 cup honey
  • •3 eggs (at room temperature)
  • •2 tsp vanilla extract
  • •1 tsp almond extract
  • •1 tsp lemon zest
  • greek yogurt frosting:
  • •2 cups Greek yogurt (strained at least 2 hours)
  • •2 tsp vanilla extract
  • •1 cup powdered sugar

instructions

  1. 1. beat frosting ingredients on medium speed until thick. refrigerate.
  2. 2. combine almond flour, baking soda and salt.
  3. 3. whisk wet ingredients.
  4. 4. add wet into dry and mix.
  5. 5. scoop batter into lined muffin tin.
  6. 6. bake at 325°F for 22-25 min.
  7. 7. frost cupcakes once cool.
