banana cookies.

makes 12 cookies

ingredients

  • •1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • •1/4 tsp baking soda
  • •1/8 tsp salt
  • •1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • •4 overripe bananas
  • •1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • •1 egg

instructions

  1. 1. combine dry ingredients.
  2. 2. mash banana with maple syrup and egg.
  3. 3. fold wet into dry.
  4. 4. scoop dough onto lined and greased cookie sheet. flatten cookies with fork.
  5. 5. bake at 350°F for ~30 min.
  6. 6. cool on wire rack.
