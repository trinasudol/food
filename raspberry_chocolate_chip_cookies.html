raspberry chocolate chip cookies.

makes 12 cookies

ingredients

  • • 2 eggs
  • • 2 tbsp honey (try coconut palm sugar next time!)
  • • 1 tbsp shredded ghee
  • • 2 tbsp vanilla extract
  • • 2 tbsp almond extract
  • • 2 cups almond flour
  • • 1/8 tsp salt
  • • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • • 1 tsp cream of tartar
  • • 1/4 cup raspberry powder
  • • chocolate chips (try adding chopped almonds next time!)

instructions

  1. 1. whisk eggs.
  2. 2. mix in honey, ghee and extracts.
  3. 3. combine dry ingredients with wet.
  4. 4. mix in chocolate chips.
  5. 5. bake at 350°F for 15-20 minutes (until they're sturdy).
